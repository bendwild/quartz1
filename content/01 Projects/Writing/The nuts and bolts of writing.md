Each one of us walks around with a story
a story you need to tell
first and foremost to yourself
your inner voice is the most important anchor to shape your future

using AI for writing demeans you
it’s a missed opportunity to share your own voice and perspective

each one of us is here a leader, whether you accept that role or not
writing is one form of your contribution to mutual learning and leadership

Ask yourself
- What story can I tell? (bring your own context into your story, be authentic)
- What is the message you want to communicate?
- What is your audience?
