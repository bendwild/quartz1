Active vs Passive Nihilism

-> the passive nihilist, when confronted with the apparent meaningless of it all, doesn't go further in the search for meaningness but sees this as an endpoint. I believe nihilism is the natural path for many individuals who grew up in the individual and overly rational western societies. However, this passive nihilism is a dangerous thing, as it leaves you vulnerable for the whims of their own ego and fears, desperately attaching themselves to anything that can help them form an identity. 

I believe the path to overcome this is active nihility. As [[@Keiji Nishitani]] said: "**A nihility that dissolves and renders null and void all paths to the transcendent demands a special means of transcendence**." Consciously building up your "belief".
