big parts of our life become about consuming the raw signifiers (ideology without ideology) of the experience, and in doing so we distance ourselves from the broader context or implications of that activity and create asense of alienation

It is not faking it till u make it anymore, in the society of the spectacle, faking it IS making it

Life is a paradox
Learning makes you feel stupid
Getting stronger makes you wek
Loving makes you feel vulnerable
This is simply the duality of life

We want to knowledgeable without feeling foolish
strong without feeling fragile
loved without being vulnerarble
We want the mountain top without the climb

Perhaps the most profound freedom comes not from having it all figured out
but from surrendering to the beautiful, maddening mystery of it all
