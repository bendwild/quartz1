# Measures of central tendency
- mean
	- sensitive to outliers
- median
- mode
	= value that occurs the most

# Measures of dispersion
- minimum / maximum
- range
- standard deviation
	= how much a value deviates from the average or mean
	- ![[Pasted image 20251019174640.png]]
- variance
	- ![[Pasted image 20251019174646.png]]
- IQR
- coefficient of variation
	- SD/mean

> [!ATTENTION] Conclusion
> - report **mean** and **SD** when distribution is normal
> - report **median** and **IQR** when distribution is non-normal
