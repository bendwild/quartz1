> [!INFO] Definition of Global Health Ethics
> = field of applied ethics, inquiry and practice including the following elements
> - critical exploration
> - interprofessional, transdisciplinary and transcultural dialogue
> - rigorous analysis
> - normative guidance in decision-making
>   
 ≠ bioethics (non-maleficence, beneficence, autonomy, justice)

# Elements of GHE
1. Critical exploration with a focus on self-reflection
	1. exploration of yourself and the (history of) the field of global health itself
	2. examination of one’s motives (human solidarity central)
	3. understanding of one’s privilege
2. Interprofessional, transdisciplinary and transcultural dialogue
	1. multiple fields (medicine, public health, bioethics…)
	2. multiple communities & cultures
	3. multiple histories
3. Rigorous analysis
	1. reflection and evolution of norms
4. Normative guidance in decision-making
	1. external agreed upon principles?

# GHE principles
1. Respect for human life
2. Human rights
3. Equity
4. Freedom
5. Democracy
6. Environmental ethics
7. Solidarity

# History & politics of GHE
→ tropical medicine, military medicine and missions → international health, international development and NGOS → global north and global south as partners in global health ethics
