**= life-long process of critical self-reflection**
- humility is central
- you will never fully be an “expert” of others identities and life experiences (≠ cultural competency)
- becoming aware of, and addressing, implicit and explicit [[bias]]

**= recognize and challenge power imbalances**
- power structures are socially and historically perpetuated
- power or lack thereof has a notable impact on health
- institutional accountability (reflection and self-critique required)

**= happens in interaction with people of different cultures and backgrounds**
- creating respect and mutual understanding
