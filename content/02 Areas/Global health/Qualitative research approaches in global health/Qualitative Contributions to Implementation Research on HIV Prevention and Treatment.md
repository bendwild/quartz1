# Reflections
[[Implementation science]]

The use of qualitative research to characterize intervention processes, let us try and include qualitative research earlier in the evidence-generating phase

Framework-driven investigation VS inductive approach
- top-down vs bottom-up
- based on a prior structure vs without predesignated framework

Focus on experiences and local explanations

Added value of qualitative approaches: 
- offer an alternative to framework-driven investigations
- offer tools for eliciting and understanding users’ subjectie experiences of interventions
- provide local explanation (an account, interpretation, or narrative that comes directly from the perspective of the people in the specific setting where the intervention is being implemented)
- leads to stronger conceptualizations

“Universal is a measurement term”

“Mechanism of effect” - what’s the why?

Qual and quant answer different questions that the other can not
