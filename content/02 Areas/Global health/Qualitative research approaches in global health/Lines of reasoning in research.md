# Types of research

--- start-multi-column: ID_suaw
```column-settings
Number of Columns: 2
Largest Column: standard
```

## Nomothetic research
- measure a phenomenon/isolate phenomenon of interest
- derive generalizable findings
- guard against measurement bias
- belief in generalizable truth/get as close to the truth as possible
	- you expect finding empirical proof
- **deductive logic** (quantitative)
- implications: 
	- need for standardized measurements that can be compared
	- need for **quantifiable responses**
	- need for statistics (as mechanism for isolating a phenomenon)

--- column-break ---

## Idiographic research
- understand **individual case** (or set of cases)
	- describe fully give the different factors, experiences, beliefs… that affect it
	- you are **not measuring**
- more suspicious of an “absolute truth”
- study in real-world context / NO isolating of a phenomenon
- **inductive logic** (qualitative)
- implications:
	- need for flexibility
	- need to understand the individual case in its specific context
	- need for **iterative adaptation**

--- end-multi-column

> [!DANGER] Qualitative aims should NEVER use the word "measure" 
> - focus on description and understanding
