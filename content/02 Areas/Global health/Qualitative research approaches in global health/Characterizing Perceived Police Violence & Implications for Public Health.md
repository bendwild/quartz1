# Reflections

[[Grounded theory]]
- qualitative analytic method designed to inductively derive theories about a topic
- 3 stages (open coding, axial coding and selective coding)

Police violence as a public health issue
- police violence increases risk of physical and mental illness
- it not only affects injectors but also non-drug users as they inherently occupy the same spaces as the former
- several reasons for excessive police violence have been given (quota’s, profiling and discrimination)
	- interestingly the limited view officers have of public spaces (single-use vs mixed use) has a profound impact

More immersive approach than Zaccharson, also took longer, more of an ethnographic approach, Zaccharson more of an outsider perspective?

More traditional way of doing qualitative research
