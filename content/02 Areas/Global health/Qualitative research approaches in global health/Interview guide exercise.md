# Peer-to-peer interviews in class next week

## Interview Domain:  Choosing to pursue a career in global health.

> [!info] 
> Interviews should focus on the personal life experiences that shaped students’ values, and the individuals who influenced them, leading to their choice to pursue this career path.

1. Brainstorm: 
	1. What is your specific research question about what led students to pursue a career in global health? What specifically do you want to know?
	2. What do you already know about choosing a career in public health? How does this shape the questions you want to ask?

2. Draft your own interview guide in class.
