# Reflections
![[Pasted image 20251006104210.png]]

Guide to general inductive approach: 
1. Condense raw textual data into a brief, summary format
2. Establish clear links between the evaluation or research objectives and the summary findings derived from the raw data
3. Develop a framework of the underlying structure of experiences or processes that are evident in the raw data

> [!attention] General inductive approach 
> - easily used and systemic set of procedures for analyzing quantitative data
> - can produce reliable and valid findings
> - CAVE not as strong as other analytic strategies (e.g.  [[Grounded theory]] for theory or model development)
