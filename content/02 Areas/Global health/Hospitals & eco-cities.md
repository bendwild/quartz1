Key messages: 
1. Use the financial and organisational assets of your hospitals to **integrate ecological changes within the surrounding community**
	- for example Cleveland hospital sharing cheap solar power electricity with surrounding low-income neighbourhoods
2. **Blue-green spaces** in cities help build a more climate-resilient health care systems and benefit healthcare facilities and patiënts
	- only 16% of urban areas have green spaces within 300m (in Europe)
	- healthcare facilities are vulnerable to climate-change impacts (such as flooding → 11% are at a potential risk)
