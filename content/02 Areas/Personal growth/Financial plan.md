# Needs
-> allocate **50%** of your budget
	- Rent / mortgage payments
	- Groceries
	- Insurance
	- Health care
	- Debt payments
	- Utilities
	- ...

# Wants
-> allocate **20%** of your budget
-> everything that isn't absolutely essential
- Hobbies / sport
- Restaurants / bars
- Fun activities
- Services (music, streaming...)
- Vacations
- Electronic gadgets
- Gifts

# [[FIRE]]
-> allocate **20%** of your budget

# [[Effective giving]]
-> allocate **10%** of your budget
