> [!important]  
> Most people with ANA will never develop a rheumatic disease - belang van pre-test probabiliteit  
  
> [!important]  
> ANA- doesn’t rule out CTD ([[Ziekte van Sjögren]] en IIM slecht 60%)  

> [!important]  
> ANA screening via indirecte immunofluorescentie op HEp-2 cells  
  
> [!important]  
> Auto-antibodies reveal the hidden truth about your patients  

> [!ATTENTION] Cardiologische verwikkelingen bij systemische inflammatoire aandoeningen
> 1. Kleplijden (SLE, APL >> RA)
> 2. Coronair lijden (RA >> SLE)
> 3. Myocardaantasting (sclerodermie > SLE & RA) -> progressieve fibrose intramyocardiale vaten
> 4. Pulmonaal arteriële [[hypertensie]] (sclerodermie > MCTD > SLE) -> progressieve fibrose pulmonaire arteriolen
> 5. [[Aneurysma]] aorta thoracalis ([[vasculitis]], [[Bindweefselaandoeningen]])
