= life-threatening organ dysfunction secondary to dysregulated host response to infection
**=/= bacteriëmie/septicemie** (= aanwezigheid van [[kiemen]] in bloedbaan)
# Klinische criteria
1. Suspected/documented infection
& 
2. Change in **[SOFA-score](https://www.mdcalc.com/calc/691/sequential-organ-failure-assessment-sofa-score)** ≥2 (sepsis-related organ failure assessment)

Sepsis bedside criteria dmv [**qSOFA-score**](https://www.mdcalc.com/calc/2654/qsofa-quick-sofa-score-sepsis )
-> identifies high-risk patients for in-hospital mortality with suspected infection outside the ICU

# Technische onderzoeken
[[ABCDE]] stabiel
-> verdere onderzoeken bij alarmtekenen
- T° >38.5C >4d
- T° >40C
- niet-pluis gevoel
- recidiverend [[braken]], hevige [[diarree]] <30m
- tekenen van meningeale prikkeling
- gedaalde algemene toestand
- dehydratie risico
- immuundepressie
- tropische reis
- chronische, ernstige comorbiditeit

Hemoculturen
-> !correct afnemen!
- voldoende volume
# Aanpak 
1. [[ABCDE]]
2. Vulling
	- CAVE fluids are not without risk!
		-> consider as drug
		- increases backward failure of heart
		- excessive interstitial fluid reduces oxygen diffusion efficiency
		- can induce metabolic changes and hemodilution
3. [[Antibiotica]]
	- vroegtijdig (<1u); hoe langer je wacht met AB, hoe slechter outcome
	- broad spectrum but keep thinking (antecedenten, context, local surveillance...)
	- toevoegen van [[clindamycine]] gunstig effect bij toxines (bv s. aureus)
	![[image-287.png]]

> [!ATTENTION] How to survive sepsis?
> 1. Act quickly upon sepsis & septic shock recognition
> 2. Minimize time to treatment (medical emergency!!)
> 3. Monitor closely for response to interventions
> 4. Communicatie sepsis status in hand-offs

# Pediatrie
-> denken in termen van orgaandysfunctie
-> **phoenix sepsis score**
	- lactaat
	- stolling
	- respiratoir falen
	- cardiovasculair falen
	- ...
(no single finding can reliably identify the severity of possible sepsis / organ failure)

> [!ATTENTION] Denk ook aan early/late neonatale sepsis
