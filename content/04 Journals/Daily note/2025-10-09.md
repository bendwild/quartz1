There is no origin
no set starting point
mainly a life governed by 
those closest to me, 
luck, 
and an unrelenting feeling of unease

unease with myself
who I am
what I stand for
where is home?

unease with the world
the dichotomy between my birthplace and my youth
the colonized and colonizers
induced suffering just to lubricate a broken machine

the uneasiness is part of who I am
and I am still defining it
as it burrows its way deeper inside my psyched
it has guided me through engagements and volunteering
but the beast was never satisfied
I thought med school would be the final meal
but it only made it hungrier
no fighting it any more

I decided
let’s set some ground rules

I follow you, out of respect for myself
you guide me, without pushing and pulling
and we see where we end up…

So me, the beast, and  a lot of luck
ended up across the ocean
attempting to settle this once and for all
although we both know this is only the beginning

The beginning of a life of humility, respect and action
that’s what we hope to achieve
not to satisfy myself
but out of a shared humanity
[[ubuntu]] or whatever you want to call it
but I decide the fucking definition
