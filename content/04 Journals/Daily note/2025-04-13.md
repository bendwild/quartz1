The world has gone to shit
full on liquid diarrhea
with maybe even a little sprinkles of blood in there
hinting at some lingering malignancy

A surprise? no...
the doomsday clock has been flirting with the number 12 way too often lately
news cycles so anhedonic they need to get medicated
outrage everywhere
it makes me numb

I need to choose my battles
you cannot help when you are dead
one boulder at a time
one must imagine

I wish I was more privileged sometimes
unaware of the many battles that need to be fought
50 shades of white and a trustfund up your sleeve
a real-life fairy tale with a racist prince
